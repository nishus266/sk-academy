    S.K Academy




    Nurturing a child is one of the greatest responsibilities of all. It requires an environment that consistently nurtures the young mind, body and spirit metamorphosing them into strong human beings, repositories of knowledge and skill capable of facing the challenges of growing up with grace.

    S.K Academy is an English medium, co-educational School that follows CBSE curriculum. The school will be upgraded to Senior Secondary (10+2) level. Teacher – Student ratio of 1:15 ensures individual attention to the pupils and caters to their different learning styles guided by highly qualified and trained teachers.

    S.K Academy is a humble endeavour to touch the heights of excellence in the field of education.



    Our Vision and Mission




    Education is the most powerful tool leading to self realization and empowerment. Our vision is to preserve and enhance the highest standards of excellence, preparing the generation next to give shape to their dreams. We train students to embrace the expanding horizons of technology and also become repository of a deep commitment to Indian ethos and culture. We strive to provide enriching experience to prepare enlightened citizens of the world sensitized to environmental concerns, feeling of nationalism, communal harmony and society at large.

    Our mission is to promote the education of the complete person integrating physical, intellectual, emotional and social balance leading to holistic development. We encourage our students to have independent sprit to explore new roles, ideas and strategies. Our mission is to instill essential life skills in our students that equip them to face the challenges of ever changing world around them and keep pace with the growth and expansion, making them global citizens in the true sense.

    Our well crafted curriculum is a wonderful blend of domains of knowledge that brings out the best in children enabling them to express confidently and creatively paving their path to success. Sports and technology are integral part of everyday learning providing students an environment to extend the learning beyond classroom taking them to higher cognitive domains of application, problem solving and analysis.
